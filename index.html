Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download Soul Deep on my iPhone?

Soul Deep is not yet available for download. Join our waitlist and you'll be notified as soon as the app launches on the App Store. The app will be compatible with iOS 14.0 and later.

How will the free trial work on iOS?

When the app launches, you'll get a 7-day free trial for the monthly plan or a 14-day free trial for the yearly plan with full access to all premium features. No credit card will be required to start - you'll only be charged if you decide to continue after the trial period ends.

How will subscription cancellation work?

You'll be able to easily cancel your subscription at any time by going to your iPhone's Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Soul Deep > Cancel Subscription. If you cancel during your free trial, you won't be charged.

Will Soul Deep work with Apple Watch?

Yes! Soul Deep will offer an iOS widget that can be added to your lock screen and home screen, allowing you to view inspirational quotes at a glance. Lock screen widgets will require iOS 16 or later, while home screen widgets will be supported on iOS 14 and later.

How much storage space will the app require?

Soul Deep is being optimized for iOS and will require less than 50MB of storage space initially. The app will download quotes as needed to save space on your device.

When will Soul Deep be available on iOS?
