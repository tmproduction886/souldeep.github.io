Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download Soul Deep on my iPhone?

Simply click any "Download on the App Store" button on this page, or search for "Soul Deep Quotes" in the App Store on your iPhone. The app is compatible with iOS 14.0 and later.

How does the free trial work on iOS?

After downloading the app, you'll get a 7-day free trial for the monthly plan or a 14-day free trial for the yearly plan with full access to all premium features. No credit card is required to start - you'll only be charged if you decide to continue after the trial period ends.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can easily cancel your subscription at any time by going to your iPhone's Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Soul Deep > Cancel Subscription. If you cancel during your free trial, you won't be charged.

Does Soul Deep work with Apple Watch?

Yes! Soul Deep offers an iOS widget that can be added to your lock screen and home screen, allowing you to view inspirational quotes at a glance. Lock screen widgets require iOS 16 or later, while home screen widgets are supported on iOS 14 and later.

How much storage space does the app require?

Soul Deep is optimized for iOS and requires less than 50MB of storage space initially. The app downloads quotes as needed to save space on your device.
